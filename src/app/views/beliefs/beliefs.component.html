Our Beliefs

THE BIBLE

We believe the Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testament to be the inspired, inerrant, and infallible Word of God.

GOD

We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons; God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.

REPENTANCE

We believe the only means to be saved from sin is through faith in Jesus Christ; His death, burial, and resurrection.

SANCTIFICATION

We believe in a distinct moment of sanctification that commences an ongoing process of yielding to God’s Word and His Spirit in order to complete development of Christ’s character in us.

HOLY SPIRIT

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as described in the Bible; it is the promise of the Father, to empower the life of the believer and the church. We believe the utterances of tongues to be the nominal occurrence of spirit baptism.

HEALING

We believe the healing of the sick is illustrated in the life and ministry of Jesus, and included in the commission of Jesus to His disciples.

THE BLESSED HOPE

We believe in the blessed Hope, which is the rapture of the Church of God, which is in Christ, at His return.
