God Wants To Use You

Nehemiah Ministry

This ministry is responsible for the assembling and breaking down of every service. Whenever there is a need in the church that requires a little bit of muscle and hard work, the Nehemiah ministry is always ready to serve.

Change For Change

Community outreach is a big deal here at New Birth. So we collect change from the members and anyone who is willing to give so we can raise money for outreach events! The volunteers of this would help collect, count, and plan the events that go with it.

The Arts

Praise and Worship, Graphics, Multimedia and more fall into the arts at the church. This is for the people who want to exercise that creative gene and give God the glory all in the same breath. Everyone under this ministry serves to enhance the worship experience at New Birth during the week and on Sundays!

Technology

We value having good tech at our church. Whether it is the latest and greatest method of using cameras, or researching the newest coding languages, we value technology A LOT. Please come and volunteer to use that great mind of yours to solve the church's next problem!

Hospitality

Are you gifted with the ability to touch people with a simple smile? Then our hospitality team needs you! They serve to make sure that everyone is comfortable in the church. From greeters to paritioners at our welcome table, you will be used!

Ascension

This is our ministry that currently reaches students at NC State, William Peace, Shaw, and Meridith college. Click here to learn more.
