Our Vision

The vision of New Birth is to develop a multi-ethnic body of disciples, Saved, Sanctified, and Spirit Filled, prepared to execute the great commission through word and deed.

Our Story

New Birth began on the campus of North Carolina A&T State University, Greensboro NC, 2010 under the leadership of Brent Murphy. God led him to Raleigh, NC. On June 19, 2016 New Birth Bible Fellowship officially planted and launched in Raleigh, NC.

Our Beliefs

We believe in God the Father, Jesus the son, and the Holy Ghost.

Our Ministries

We have many dynamic ministries that function to save souls, change lives, and transform communities.
